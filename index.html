Automatic Tourist Planner

This application aims to generate a personalised travel timetable automatically. After agreeing with the terms and conditions and permitting us to view your Instagram photos, this app will present you with an activity plan for a holiday in Malta. There is a 50% chance that the timetable is personalised based on your Instagram profile. After that, rate the result on a scale of 1 to 5.

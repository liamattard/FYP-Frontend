Automatic Tourist Planner

After agreeing with the terms and conditions and permitting us to view information about you by connecting this app with your social media profiles (Facebook and Instagram), you will be asked to select your desired activity moderation and the number of days you are travelling. this app will then present you with two timetables for a holiday in Malta. There will be a button at the bottom of the page to select which timetable appeals more to your liking
